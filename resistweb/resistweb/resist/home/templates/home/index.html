About Resist


In recent months, all across the world people have been coming out of their homes to protest against police brutality and for things like the Black Lives Matter Movement and women's rights and equality.


We were inspired by this and wanted to create an website and app that would help people find nearby protests and rallies where they could express their voice. Generation Z has been very vocal on their opinions and many peers I know are protesting along, posting images on social media (black out Tuesday on Instagram).
